Our Story

Niyantri Cafe began as a small, neighborhood coffee spot in Hyderabad. We source ethical beans, roast with care, and craft beverages to honor classic techniques while embracing modern tastes. Our space is designed to feel like a home away from home—a place to linger with friends, study, or simply savor a quiet moment with a great cup.
